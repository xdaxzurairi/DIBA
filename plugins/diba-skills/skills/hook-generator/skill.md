# Skill: hook-generator

## Tujuan
Generate 5 scroll-stopping opening hooks yang boleh digunakan untuk LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, email subject lines, artikel, atau video scripts. Setiap hook disertakan dengan explanation kenapa ia berfungsi secara psikologi.

## Cara Guna
Abam provide:
- **Topik/Content:** Apa yang nak disampaikan
- **Platform:** LinkedIn / Twitter / Instagram / Email / Video / General
- **Target audience:** Siapa yang nak reach
- **Tone:** Professional / Casual / Bold / Inspirational / Educational
- **Optional:** Angle atau emotion yang nak trigger

## 7 Formula Hook Yang Terbukti

DIBA akan pilih yang sesuai dengan topik:

### 1. Contrarian Hook
*Bantah conventional wisdom*
> "Stop [sesuatu yang semua orang buat]. It's killing your [hasil]."
> "Everyone tells you to [X]. They're wrong."

### 2. Curiosity Gap Hook
*Buka loop yang otak terpaksa tutup*
> "I made [result] doing the opposite of what everyone said."
> "The reason most [people/businesses] fail at [X] has nothing to do with [obvious reason]."

### 3. Bold Statement / Hot Take
*Statement berani yang boleh setuju atau tak setuju*
> "[Common belief] is the biggest lie in [industry]."
> "Unpopular opinion: [Statement yang surprising tapi boleh diargue]"

### 4. Number / Specificity Hook
*Nombor konkrit beri kredibiliti*
> "I spent [X hours/days/months] doing [X]. Here's what I learned."
> "[X] things I wish I knew before [common experience]."

### 5. Story Hook
*Mulakan dengan scene atau moment*
> "3 months ago, I was [situation]. Today, [transformed situation]."
> "She looked at me and said: '[Quote yang intriguing]'"

### 6. Problem Agitation Hook
*Name the pain*
> "If you've ever [relatable frustration], this is for you."
> "You're not [failing/struggling] because you're lazy. You're doing [X] wrong."

### 7. Stakes / FOMO Hook
*Buat orang rasa rugi kalau tak baca*
> "Most people won't know this until it's too late."
> "This [insight/opportunity] disappears in [timeframe]."

## Platform-Specific Hook Rules

**LinkedIn:**
- Hook mesti complete dalam 2 baris sebelum "...see more"
- Avoid clickbait yang obvious
- Professional curiosity works best: career, money, productivity, leadership

**Twitter/X:**
- Hook IS the tweet (kalau standalone)
- Bold, polarizing, atau funny
- Under 100 chars jika boleh

**Instagram:**
- Baris pertama sahaja visible before "more"
- Must create strong visual atau emotional curiosity
- Emoji boleh enhance (tapi jangan over)

**Email Subject Line:**
- 40-60 chars maximum
- Personalisation boost open rates
- Question marks dan numbers work well

## Psikologi Di Sebalik Hooks Yang Berfungsi

1. **Curiosity gap** — otak manusia hate incomplete loops
2. **Identity signal** — "ini untuk orang macam kau"
3. **Loss aversion** — takut miss out lebih kuat dari desire untuk gain
4. **Social proof** — "ramai orang salah paham ini"
5. **Contrarianism** — agree tapi rasa special sebab "tahu lebih"
6. **Specificity** — nombor konkrit = lebih credible
7. **Story brain** — manusia hardwired untuk follow narrative
